## Adding a race to your Google Calendar

RacePals lets you add a race to your own Google Calendar — just tap
**"Add to Google Calendar"** on any race page.

The first time you use it, Google will show a screen that says
**"Google hasn't verified this app."** That's completely normal for an app
that's still in testing — it doesn't mean anything is wrong, and RacePals only
ever adds the event to *your* calendar.

To continue:
1. Tap **Advanced** (small link, usually bottom-left).
2. Tap **Go to RacePals (unsafe)**.
3. Tap **Continue / Allow** to let it add the event.

You'll only have to do this once. After that, races are added with a single tap.

If you'd rather not connect your calendar, just don't tap that button —
everything else in the app works without it. 🏃

> The "unverified app" warning only appears for the calendar feature, not when
> you sign in to RacePals. Verification with Google is planned before the public
> launch; until then this one-time warning is expected.
