RacePals — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026

RacePals ("the app", "we", "us") is an app for UK runners to discover races and parkruns, log the events they attend, write reviews, and connect with other runners as "Pals". This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, and the rights you have over it. We are committed to handling your data in line with the UK GDPR.

Who is responsible for your data

The data controller is [YOUR FULL NAME]. If you have any questions, or want to exercise any of your rights, contact us at [YOUR CONTACT EMAIL].

What data we collect

  • Account details — when you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address and profile photo from your Google account.
  • Profile — a display name, optional bio, and an optional profile photo you upload.
  • Your activity — the races and parkruns you mark as going to or attended, the reviews and ratings you write, and your "Pals" connections.
  • Location you type — when you search "near a town", we use the text you enter to find nearby races. We do not track your device's GPS location in the background.

Why we use it, and our legal basis

  • To provide the app's core features — discovering races, logging attendance, showing reviews, and connecting you with Pals. Legal basis: performance of a contract (providing the service you signed up for).
  • To keep the app secure and working correctly. Legal basis: our legitimate interests.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell it.

Who we share it with

RacePals runs on Google Firebase (authentication, database and file storage). Google processes this data on our behalf as a sub-processor. Some of your information is visible to other signed-in users by design — for example your display name, photo, public reviews, and the races you attend. Reviews you mark "Pals only" are visible just to you and your Pals.

How long we keep it

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, your personal data is removed (see below).

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict its processing. In particular:

  • Delete your account and data — in the app, go to the Me tab and tap Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, photo, reviews, race history, Pals and activity.
  • If you cannot access the app, email us at [YOUR CONTACT EMAIL] and we will delete your data within 30 days.

You also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Children

RacePals is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top shows when it last changed.
