# RacePal v0.2.0-beta — Full Redesign 🗺️

## What's new

- **Map is now home** — 884 UK parkruns (green), 1,190 UK race events (orange), and community-added races (yellow), all on one map with search and filters
- **"I'm doing this"** works everywhere — tap any pin, hit the button, and it's on your calendar
- **New Calendar tab** — see your upcoming and past races, plus everything your pals have signed up for. List view or month view.
- **Find Pals** 🔍 — search runners by name and follow them (Me → Pals → search icon, top right)
- **Pals** = people who follow you back (mutual follows)
- **Redesigned reviews** — star rating + "Would you recommend?" Races with ≥80% recommends (min 10 reviews) earn a ⚡
- **Edit & delete your reviews**

## How to install

1. On your Android phone, download `app-release.apk` below
2. Open the file — allow "install from unknown sources" if asked
3. Sign in with Google and you're in!

## How to connect with friends

1. Both install the app and sign in
2. Go to **Me → Pals → search icon (top right)**
3. Search your friend's name (as it appears on their Google account), tap **Follow**
4. When they follow you back, you become **Pals** and see each other's races in the Calendar
